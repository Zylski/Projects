The Shinra Electric Power Company

The Shinra electric power company has been around since 1951 following the end of World War 2. Shinra started off as a weapons manufacturer and automobile manufacturer. Since then Shinra has been researching Mako energy as a power source and this has lead to a breakthrough in the realm of energy.

Today Shinra has branched out into a multi department company with a presence in almost every nation. Many cutting edge projects are being undertaken by Shinra including a cutting edge space program! Shinra will continue to lead the energy revolution of tomorrow.
